Create your own string train in Javascript

Rules and Symbol

- The train must have minimum 3 and maximum 5 elements.
- Locomotive can go at the beginning and\or at the end of the train.
- You can't fill a full train with cargo cars.


  • Locomotive: H
  • Passenger: P
  • Restaurant: R
  • Cargo: C
