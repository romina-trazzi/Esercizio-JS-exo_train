Create your own string train in Javascript

Rules and Symbol

  1. The train must have minimum 3 and maximum 5 elements.
  2. You can choose either a People train or a Cargo Train:
    - People trains have Passenger and Restaurant cars
    - Cargo trains have only Cargo cars.
  3. Locomotive can go at the beginning and\or at the end of People trains, but only at the beginning of Cargo trains.
  4. You can't fill a full train with cargo cars. The first car must be a locomotive.
  • Locomotive: H
  • Passenger: P
  • Restaurant: R
  • Cargo: C
